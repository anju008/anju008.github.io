The Kooples × Kleep AI - Sizing Audit

MVV

Mission: Transform sizing uncertainty from a conversion killer into a trust builder through AI-powered personalization.
Vision: Every shopper finds their perfect fit on the first try, eliminating returns and building lifetime loyalty.
Values: Data-driven decisions, customer-centric design, measurable impact.

The Complete 5-Email Flow

Email 1 - Size Reassurance & Order Confirmation
Goal: Reduce post-order anxiety immediately
The first 30 minutes after a €500+ purchase are the highest anxiety moment, confirm the size choice and set expectations.
Email 2 - Pre-Delivery Fit Preparation
Goal: Prepare the shopper for the first try-on moment
The biggest return trigger is surprise, the jacket "feels different than expected." This email eliminates that surprise before it happens.
Email 3 - Post-Delivery Satisfaction Signal
Goal: Collect a satisfaction signal and identify at-risk customers
Collect a satisfaction signal (NPS or quick feedback), identify at-risk customers early, and begin the cross-sell journey for customers who are happy.
Email 4 - Return Prevention Window
Goal: Catch undecided shoppers before they return
Most brands ignore the return window until after it closes. Sending a targeted email 2 days before the window closes catches undecided shoppers at the exact decision moment.
Email 5 - Repeat Purchase & Size Lock-In
Goal: Convert first-time buyers into repeat customers
Use the confirmed size data from Kleep as a personalization signal to drive repeat purchases.

Margin-at-Risk Analysis

Price = risk magnitude. The Kooples Jackets & Coats range from €225 to €795 per item. At this AOV, one wrong size does not just trigger a return, it erases the entire gross margin on that transaction.

Sizing format = conversion barrier. The Kooples uses French numeric sizing (FR 34–46) instead of XS–XL. For any non-French shopper including UK, US, and German customers, this is a mental friction event that forces external research before clicking Add to Cart.

Cut complexity = return multiplier. Leather jackets run fitted (size up if between sizes). Blazers require sizing up a full UK size. Coats need a layering allowance. One generic size chart cannot solve this. Kleep's AI is the only tool that accounts for garment-specific construction.

That's why I'm framing it as insurance for high-value conversions, not a feature.

GA4 Measurement Layer

The Kooples Jackets & Coats category runs from €225 to €795. At that price point, sizing uncertainty is not a UX problem, it's a margin problem. Industry data shows 64% of fashion returns are size-related, so every return in this category costs the brand the full gross margin on a €500+ item.

The Kooples adds two layers of complexity on top of that: French numeric sizing creates a mental translation barrier for international shoppers, and the cut, particularly leather jackets and structured blazers requires specific guidance that a standard size chart cannot give. Kleep's AI is the only tool that personalises this at the garment level.

Post-Purchase CRM Safety Net

A shopper who buys a €595 Kooples leather jacket has 14 days to return it for free. If the jacket feels "different" when it arrives, slightly snug at the shoulders, stiffer than expected then they will return it unless someone has already told them that is normal for this cut. The CRM is the only channel that can deliver that message at exactly the right moment. Every avoided return at this price point protects both margin and a potential lifetime customer.

Email Flow Implementation

DAY 0 · TRIGGER: ORDER CONFIRMED
Subject: "Your order is confirmed, and here's why you chose the right size"
"Thank you for your order. You selected FR [size] based on your Kleep recommendation, this is the right fit for your body measurements and the cut of this jacket."
DAY 2 · TRIGGER: DISPATCH CONFIRMED
Subject: "Your jacket ships today, here's the 3-step fit check"
"Your jacket is on its way and will arrive in [X] days. Before your first try-on, here's what to expect from this specific garment..."
DAY 7 · TRIGGER: 7 DAYS AFTER ESTIMATED DELIVERY
Subject: "How does it fit? [Name]"
"We hope your jacket has arrived and you love it. We have one quick question: how does the fit feel?"
DAY 12 · TRIGGER: 12 DAYS AFTER DELIVERY
Subject: "Still deciding? Your return window closes in 2 days"
"Your 14-day return window closes on [date]. Before it does, we want to make sure you are completely happy with your fit."
DAY 60 · TRIGGER: NO RETURN INITIATED IN 60 DAYS
Subject: "You found your Kooples size - here's the new season in FR [size]"
"You kept your jacket - which means you found your Kooples fit. We've saved your size (FR [size]) so your next purchase is just as easy."
