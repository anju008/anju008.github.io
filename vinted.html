Vinted Realities

An experimental storytelling concept for a fashion resale platform aimed at Gen Z engagement and brand affinity.

Objective

  • Drive top‑of‑funnel awareness and social sharing among Gen Z.
  • Showcase sustainability and circular fashion through narrative.
  • Test short‑form video concepts for paid and organic distribution.

Role

  • Concept development and creative direction.
  • Script and storyboard; asset planning and coordination.
  • Post‑production feedback loop and performance notes.

Creative

Short, punchy scenes stitched around the idea of "realities"—how preloved pieces carry stories across owners. Built to be modular for TikTok/Reels, with hooks in the first 2 seconds and bold captions for sound‑off viewing.

Results & Next Steps

  • Prototype stage: use this as a test asset for A/B creative iterations (thumbnail, hook line, CTA).
  • Measure: 3‑sec views, 95% completion rate, profile clicks, saves/shares.
  • Next: build a 3‑video series; layer creator collabs and UGC duets.
