Loro Piana Brand Study

Comprehensive brand, market, and competitive analysis for luxury fashion house Loro Piana, including strategic positioning and communication recommendations.

  • Product scope: Ready‑to‑wear, leather goods, shoes, accessories, homewear, fabrics; womenswear ~60%, menswear ~40%.
  • Pricing snapshots: Cashmere €380–€16.5k; Vicuña €1.8k–€34k; “Gift of Kings” €850–€14k; Leather €355–€5k; Baby Cashmere €6.2k–€26.1k; Silk €4k–€9.8k.
  • Go‑to‑market: selective boutiques (e.g., Av. Montaigne), premium service & events; strong online presence and multi‑channel consistency.
  • Market context: Luxury goods revenue projected ~US$418.9B by 2028; France luxury fashion ~US$6.56B (2023) with ~83% offline sales.
  • Opportunities: Asia presence (90+ stores) but no India store; youth segment growth; balance offline & digital experiences.
